# Sourdough starter

A low-maintenance levain feeding schedule for daily sourdough bread.

## Feeding ratios

| Flour (g) | Water (g) | Hydration |
|---|---|---|
| 500 | 350 | 70% |
| 500 | 375 | 75% |
| 500 | 400 | 80% |

## Steps

1. Discard all but 50g of starter.
2. Add 50g flour and 50g water (100% hydration) for daily maintenance.
3. Feed 12 hours before baking using the ratios above for a stiffer dough.
4. Keep at room temperature, feed once daily.
